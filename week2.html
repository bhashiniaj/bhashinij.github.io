Week 2: PV Panel Selection, MPPT & Boost Converter Design

Phase: Phase 1: Literature Review & Planning

Status: ✅ Done

What We Did This Week

  • Selected a flexible Mono-crystalline Silicon (Mono-Si) solar panel for higher efficiency (20%), lightweight structure, flexibility for vehicle mounting, and low degradation rate.
  • Calculated rooftop output power: P = A × G × η = 1.5 × 1000 × 0.20 = 300W (1.5m² panel, 1kW/m² irradiance, 20% efficiency).
  • Calculated daily energy: E = 300W × 5h = 1.5 kWh/day using Sri Lanka's average 5 peak sun hours.
  • Calculated added EV range: 1500Wh ÷ 80Wh/km = 18.75 km/day for a typical three-wheeler.
  • Selected Perturb & Observe (P&O) MPPT algorithm — continuously adjusts boost converter duty cycle to maintain the panel's maximum power point.
  • Completed full boost converter parametric design: Dmax=62.5%, Iin_max=16.67A, L=45µH, C=163µF, fsw=50kHz.
