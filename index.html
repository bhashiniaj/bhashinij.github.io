About

Profile

I am a final-year undergraduate student in the Department of Electrical & Electronic Engineering at the University of Peradeniya. My academic journey has developed a strong foundation in circuit theory, power electronics, control systems, and embedded systems design.

My primary area of interest lies in photovoltaic (PV) systems and solar energy harvesting — particularly the power electronic interfaces that enable efficient energy conversion from renewable sources. I am drawn to the challenge of making clean energy solutions practical and accessible in the local context of Sri Lanka.

My Final Year Project focuses on a PV integrated electric vehicle charging system — combining MPPT-controlled DC–DC power conversion, LiFePO₄ battery management, and supercapacitor buffering to enable solar-powered charging for light electric vehicles.
