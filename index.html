Final Year Project

PV Integrated EV Charging System

This FYP designs a solar-powered electric vehicle charging system using a 300W flexible mono-crystalline PV array, a custom DC-DC boost converter with MPPT control, a supercapacitor buffer, and a 48V LiFePO₄ battery pack — targeting light EVs such as e-bicycles and three-wheelers in Sri Lanka.

Project Goals

Three Core Objectives

Solar Energy Harvesting

Size a 1.5m² flexible mono-Si PV panel (300W) with MPPT to generate 1.5 kWh/day under Sri Lanka's ~5 peak sun hours, providing +18.75 km daily EV range.

DC-DC Boost Converter

Design a non-isolated boost converter (L=45µH, C=163µF, f=50kHz) stepping up 18–36V PV input to stable 48V bus, with 90–95% estimated efficiency.

Smart Battery Management

Integrate a 48V LiFePO₄ pack (15S, 6Ah, 288Wh) with EDLC supercapacitor buffer — absorbing current spikes and extending battery life by 20–30%.
